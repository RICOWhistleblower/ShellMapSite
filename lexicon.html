Lexicon Categories

⚖️ Legal Manipulation
TermDefinitionExample
Strategic AmbiguityVague language crafted to obscure legal boundaries or truth“In accordance with applicable guidelines”
Pretextual CompliancePerforming regulatory rituals while evading true accountabilityRunning ethics modules with no internal enforcement
🧠 Psychological Undermining
TermDefinitionExample
Gaslight LoopRecurring denial of provable facts to destabilize perception“That never happened—are you sure?”
Credibility SiphonSubtle casting of doubt on a source to erode their authority“She’s passionate but tends to exaggerate”
💼 Procedural Weaponization
TermDefinitionExample
Process FogFlooding a matter with bureaucracy to obstruct resolution“Submit this to legal, ethics, then admin—twice”
Selective EscalationTriggering procedures only when beneficial to institutional controlLaunching an audit only once the whistleblower speaks
🚪 Gatekeeping & Isolation
TermDefinitionExample
Access ThinningSubtle restriction of resources or visibility to isolate dissentersRevoking shared drive access after speaking out
Credential StaggeringDelays or blocks in renewing or issuing qualifications“We're just waiting on approvals—it’s standard”
🎭 Reputation Erosion
TermDefinitionExample
Whispers of DiscreditInformal slander shared peer-to-peer to fracture trust“I’ve heard she’s had trouble before”
Integrity SubversionFraming honest behavior as erratic or suspicious“She’s obsessed with policies—bit unstable maybe?”
🔧 Systemic Sabotage
TermDefinitionExample
Tool AttritionGradual removal or corruption of tools critical to functionBuggy platform updates after reporting issues
Record SuppressionErasure or obstruction of historical data to distort truthDeleted logs from internal whistleblower hotline
📊 Data Distortion
TermDefinitionExample
Metric GroomingManipulating data presentation to mask dysfunction“We only report resolved cases, not open ones”
Insight StarvationObfuscating or limiting access to datasets that reveal misconduct“We can’t release the raw logs, just the summary”
📬 Communication Control
TermDefinitionExample
Message WashingOver-editing messages to strip context or intent“Let’s remove that last line before publishing”
Reply BlackoutIntentional delay or silence in digital communicationsNo reply for weeks, forcing oral updates
💣 Disruption Tactics
TermDefinitionExample
Meeting MinefieldStrategically stacked meetings to overload dissentersThree back-to-back calls with contradictory goals
Operation FractureSudden reshuffling of workgroups during testimony“You’ve been reassigned to another team starting tomorrow”
🪤 Entrapment Patterns
TermDefinitionExample
Bait WorkflowAssigning tasks designed to induce procedural violations“You're expected to use that deprecated form—it’s fine”
Intent InflationPresuming malicious motive from ambiguous behaviorReading editing timestamps as evidence of concealment
🚫 Content Suppression
TermDefinitionExample
Platform SmotheringFlagging or shadowbanning posts that expose misconductDisappearing whistleblower threads after repost
Keyword FreezingSilently disabling search terms that lead to sensitive materialSearch for “ethics violation” returns empty results
